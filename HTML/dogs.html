☀ Dogs as Pets ☀

Pros

  • Are loyal, loving, and social creatures
  • Are known to improve mental and heart health
  • Can add meaning and sense of purpose to your life
  • Can help you pick up girls
  • Home safety

Cons

  • High energy and maintenance
  • Greater increased living expenses compared to other pets
  • Requires a lot of time
  • Care when you're away from home
Dogs are great companions to us. They are known as man's best friend after all. Having a dog is a great way to increase liveliness in your house. Dogs are known to show unconditional love and improve health. As there are a variety of different breeds, there will be more choices for people to choose. If you want a loving, active friend, then a dog is the choice for you.
