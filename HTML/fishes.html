★ Fishes as Pets ★

Pros

  • Cost is low other than buying and setting up a fish tank
  • Low maintenance, only need to be fed daily and have water cleaned weekly
  • Watching fish swim is fun
  • Less involved compared to other pets

Cons

  • Short lifespan
  • Potential for fish tank to leak and flood
  • Need to monitor water temperature and cleanliness
  • Need to replace water to prevent algae buildup
Having a pet fish is a great choice if you are a busy person and have limited time to interact with pets. A fish will give you a sense of responsibility and companionship without having to put in as much effort as other pets. A pet fish is a great choice if you like peace and want a friend.
