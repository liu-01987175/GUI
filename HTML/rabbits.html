☀ Rabbits as Pets ☀

Pros

  • Are cute
  • Are very playful and make great companion animals
  • Typically quiet
  • Surprisingly intelligent, can learn commands like sit

Cons

  • High maintenance and needs specialized care
  • Can die from fright
  • Need to clean litter box often
  • Very messy and and needs lots of space
Rabbits are very cute and playful. They make great companions for those looking for a new and unique experience. They need careful supervision and are very high maintenance but are unique and bring a fresh understanding of caretaking.
